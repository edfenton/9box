Name: Ed Fenton
 Project Title: 9-Box
 Project Stack: MERN
 GitHub Repo Link: https://github.com/edfenton/9box

 Project Description: 9-box is a talent management tool that allows people managers to assess the potential and performance of their direct reports for the purposes of development planning.  The key benefit is the dashboard visualization that will allow the ratings to be made relative to other team members to ensure correct callibration.
